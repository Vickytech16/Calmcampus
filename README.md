
# **CalmCampus** 🎓🧘‍♂️  
A **mental wellness companion** designed to help students track, manage, and improve their **mental health** in academic life.  

## 🌟 **Features**  
✅ **Mood Tracking** – Log daily emotions and identify patterns.  
✅ **Mindfulness Exercises** – Guided meditation and relaxation techniques.  
✅ **Journaling** – A private space to express thoughts and feelings.  
✅ **Community Support** – Connect with peers for shared experiences.  
✅ **Resource Library** – Articles and tips on stress management and self-care.  
✅ **Emergency Assistance** – Quick access to helplines and professional support.  

## 🛠 **Tech Stack**  
- **Flutter** (for cross-platform mobile development)  
- **Firebase** (for authentication, data storage, and analytics)  
- **Dart** (programming language)
